You can add barcodes in the markup, by adding the barcode class to an HTML element, containing the barcode text. View source for the markup. No images, no server-side stuff. Just HTML, Javascript and CSS.

  • code128-base2.js (the base2 version of the script)
  • code128.js (the script)
  • code128.css (the css)

The barcodes have been tested with onlinebarcodereader.com and zxing.org.
