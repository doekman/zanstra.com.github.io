postTool

When developing and testing ASP-pages, I thought it would be handy to easily generate HTTP-Requests with a POST body. Just take a form, fill out the parameters and their values of the to-be-tested ASP-page. Very easy for those hard or impossible to reproduce cases, like an ordernumber which doesn't exist.

The result is postTool.htm. I also made a tool to check which parameters a page submits. This one is called postTool.asp.

Just as a spin-off, I made saveForm, which saves the values of the inputs of a form to a cookie and gets 'em back. This can easily be used via an IFRAME and one line of code to initialize it.

Quick start

  • Choose example (below)
  • Fill out one or more parameters with their values
  • Leave action on 'postTool.asp'
  • Click on submit and see what happens.

Handy for web-mail

Not really, but it's possible. Just go to hotmail.com Save the startpage on your desktop, change the action-URL to http://huizen.dds.nl/~olrik/javascriptman/postTool/postTool.asp. (download JS or VBS asp version) Load this (altered) page in your browser, submit it. At postTool.asp, you can save the form-values to a cookie (sorry, doesn't work at the moment).

Now, go back to postTool.htm: load the cookie and you have a one-button logon. What people do, to keep themselves busy ;-)
